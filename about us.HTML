The problem we have identified

It is very difficult and time consuming navigating different institutions websites to find the exact information you need.
This problem leads to students not being informed properly about information regarding their studies which has a negative chain reaction in their work.

Solution to the problem

We are developing a ONE-STOP user friendly website that will make relevant information easy to consume.
Users will be able to access, read and extract information relating to their searches.
Content such as images, videos and voice recordings will be put into place to make it easier for students.
